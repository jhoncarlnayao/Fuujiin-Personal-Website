About Me

I'm Fuujin

I'm a 4th-year student at the University of Mindanao in Davao City with a strong passion for web development and digital design. Over the years, I have explored different areas such as Figma designs, pixel art, and poster creation. I enjoy taking on personal projects because they allow me to experiment, learn new technologies, and challenge myself creatively.

Most of my projects are self-initiated, giving me the freedom to explore new concepts and improve my technical skills. I also take on small commissions, which help me understand client needs and deliver functional, visually appealing results. By combining development and design, I aim to create projects that are both user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing.

Beyond coding and design, I enjoy learning about new tools and trends in the tech industry, constantly pushing myself to grow as a developer and designer. Every project I work on, whether personal or commissioned, is an opportunity to explore ideas, solve problems, and create something meaningful. I believe in learning by doing, and this approach has allowed me to build a versatile skill set that continues to evolve.
